X2 Philips 4ft 36w 865 T8 Fluorescent Tube - Daylight

The Philips 4ft 36w 865 T8 Fluorescent Tube, also known as the "Daylight" tube, is a lighting solution designed to mimic natural daylight. With a length of 4 feet and a power consumption of 36 watts, this tube offers a bright and efficient illumination suitable for various indoor environments. The "865" designation indicates its color temperature, with 865 representing a daylight color temperature of around 6500 Kelvin. This high color temperature makes it ideal for spaces where clear visibility and color accuracy are essential, such as offices, schools, and retail settings. Additionally, the T8 design ensures compatibility with standard T8 fluorescent fixtures, making it easy to retrofit existing lighting systems. Overall, the Philips Daylight fluorescent tube provides a reliable and energy-efficient lighting solution for spaces that require bright, natural-looking illumination.
